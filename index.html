Daily Focus (Alpha)

Daily Focus (Alpha)

Daily Focus is a time block daily planner app. Time blocking is the concept of planning out your day by giving every minute of your time a specific job. This helps me make a realistic, effective schedule. The concept is based off of Cal Newport's Daily Time Block Planner, and it's an essential part of my focused work routine. Unlike a traditional daily planner, this app encourages revision when plans change. If your schedule changes, copy a time block to a new column and re-plan your day from there. Review what you planned versus what really happened at the end of your day. Use that knowledge to make a better plan the next day. Avoid overwhelm and burnout with time blocking. This app is a work in progress.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, SQLite, Hotwire
